Leading Lines

Information

  • Leading lines are vectors or lines that draw the viewer's eye a certain direction or to a certain point in the composition.
  • They can point to a focal point in an image.
  • They can be formed by existing lines in the scene result from your position relative to the focal point or object you want to emphasize.
  • It doesn't have to be a literal line. I can be elements lines up to suggest a line.
  • Diagonal lines can add energy and sometimes tension to an image.
